The warmest and most distinctive pairing. Fraunces has optical quirks and ink-trap details that feel handcrafted. Outfit is friendly and geometric. The most "Cassette" of the three.
My lean: Pairing 03
Fraunces was designed specifically to evoke warmth, nostalgia, and a sense of the handmade — it even has optical size variants that get more quirky and expressive at display sizes. It's distinctive without being precious. Outfit as the UI font keeps everything readable and modern on small mobile screens. Together they feel cozy and considered, which is exactly what Cassette should feel like.
